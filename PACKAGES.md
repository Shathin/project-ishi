  - **Null Safety**: ✅
  - **URL**: https://pub.dev/packages/sembast

  - **Description**: Flutter plugin for reading and writing simple key-value pairs.
  - **Support**: Android, iOS, MacOS, Web, Linux, **Windows**

  - **Null Safety**: ✅
